I'm an incoming Ph.D. student in Northwestern University's Media, Techonology, and Society , and I'm also a master's student in UChicago's MACSS program.

I'm broadly interested in misinformation, science of science (dark pattern), online democracy, social media analysis, social network analysis, NLP, computational social science, and AI for social good.

At UChicago, I'm working in the Knowledge Lab with Prof. James Evans on an NSF-funded Misinformation Project.

Previously, I graduated from Emory University with B.S. in Mathematics/Computer Science and Psychology/Linguistics. At Emory, I worked in the Concept Mining Lab with Prof. Phillip Wolff on investigating LLM(large language model)'s knowledge about physical world.
